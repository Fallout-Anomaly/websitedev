# Fallen World

**Fallen World** is a true hardcore survival modlist for Fallout 4. With over 100,000 downloads, it has established itself as the #1 hardcore survival experience for players who want every choice, every battle, and every step into the wasteland to feel meaningful.

Easily installed with Wabbajack, Fallen World weaves together hundreds of carefully selected mods into a cohesive package that transforms the Commonwealth into a savage and unforgiving land where survival is never assured.

---

<TerminalCard title="The Fallen World Experience">

### What makes Fallen World unique?

*   **Deadly Combat**: Strategic systems that make every encounter tactical and lethal.
*   **Core Survival**: Hunger, thirst, injuries, and radiation demand constant attention.
*   **Expanded Content**: Hand-picked weapons, companions, enemies, and regions.
*   **Enhanced Settlements**: Modern visual upgrades and expanded building features.
*   **Unified Experience**: Seamlessly integrated mods that function as a single game.

</TerminalCard>

## The Pillars of Fallen World

The Fallen World experience is built upon several foundational modules that work in harmony.

<TerminalCard title="Survival & Realism">

*   **[Advanced Needs 76](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/58440)** — The comprehensive backbone of the survival experience, managing hunger, thirst, sleep, and hygiene.
*   **[MAIM Redux](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/72235)** — A realistic medical system where wounds require specific treatments like bandages, tourniquets, and surgery.
*   **RADS** — A complete radiation overhaul that turns environmental toxicity into a lethal, escalating threat. **Custom built** by our team.
*   **[Immersive Animation Framework](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/50555)** — Adds hundreds of first-person animations for consuming items, ensuring you are vulnerable while healing.

</TerminalCard>

<TerminalCard title="Combat & Ballistics">

*   **[True Damage](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/66071)** — A caliber-based damage system that ensures lethality is determined by the round fired, not artificial weapon levels.
*   **[Munitions](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/66051)** — Expands the ammunition pool with lore-friendly calibers and realistic ballistic properties.
*   **[SCOURGE](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/60917)** — Randomizes enemy health and stats within logical ranges, removing the predictability of "leveled" enemies.
*   **[VAFS Redux](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/36519)** — Offers a tactical bullet-time alternative to VATS, perfect for a real-time survival setting.
*   **[Bastion](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/75184)** — A Power Armor overhaul that focuses on locational damage and realistic entry/exit mechanics.

</TerminalCard>

<TerminalCard title="Major Quests & Story Content">

*   **[America Rising 2](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/75767)** — A massive, fully-voiced Enclave questline that integrates seamlessly into the main story of Fallout 4.
*   **[Tales from the Commonwealth](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/8704)** — Adds hundreds of new NPCs, dozens of quests, and a huge amount of world-building to the base game.
*   **[Boon Island](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/84449)** — Explore a custom-built island in the Isles of New England series with its own narrative.
*   **[Misery Island](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/87983?tab=files)** — Introducing a dangerous new worldspace with dozens of unique locations.
*   **[Vault 117](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/96501)** — A high-quality horror quest mod that takes you deep into a custom-built vault.
*   **[In The Flesh](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/78628?tab=description)** — A psychological horror quest experience centered around a mysterious location.

</TerminalCard>

<TerminalCard title="Technical Foundation & UI">

*   **[Previsibines Repair Pack (PRP)](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/46403)** — A vital technical framework that fixes game-wide visibility and stability issues.
*   **[FallUI Suite](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/48758)** — A total modernization of every interface element, from the HUD to the Pip-Boy.
*   **[Wabbajack](https://www.wabbajack.org/)** — The automation tool used to install this complex 600+ mod setup with a single click.

</TerminalCard>
