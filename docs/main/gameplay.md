# Gameplay guide

Fallout Anomaly is a comprehensive transformation of Fallout 4, moving away from action-RPG tropes and toward a hardcore survival simulation. The Commonwealth is unforgiving, resources are scarce, and every decision carries weight.

This guide provides a high-level overview of the major systems you will encounter. Use the sidebar or the links below for deep-dive details on specific mechanics.

---

## The Core Systems

### [Survival & Realism](/docs/main/gameplay/survival)
The heartbeat of the Anomaly experience. You must manage complex needs including hunger, thirst, sleep, and hygiene. Healing is no longer instantaneous; wounds require specific medical attention, and radiation is an escalating lethal poison that demands immediate treatment.

### [Combat & Progression](/docs/main/gameplay/combat)
Experience a lethal combat environment powered by caliber-based damage and randomized enemy scaling. Character growth is decoupled from traditional SPECIAL stats and tied to the S7 Skills system, where your abilities improve through direct use and specialized training.

### [Controls & Interface](/docs/main/gameplay/controls)
Navigate the wasteland with a minimalist, context-sensitive HUD and a suite of quick-access menus. Learn the tactical keybinds for weapon maintenance, medical self-administration, and atmospheric survival.

---

## Strategy for Success

### [Tips & Strategies](/docs/main/gameplay/tips)
Success in Anomaly depends on preparation rather than reflexes. From pre-medicating before a breach to managing your ammunition weight and maintaining your gear, survival requires a tactical mindset.

:::info Customization
Almost every system in Fallout Anomaly can be tailored to your preference. Use the **Mod Configuration Menu (MCM)** in-game to adjust the difficulty, visibility, and frequency of various survival mechanics to suit your playstyle.
:::
