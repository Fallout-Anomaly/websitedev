# Tips & strategies

Survival in the Anomaly isn't just about fast reflexes—it's about preparation, knowledge, and patience. Here are the essential strategies to help you navigate the wasteland.

---

<TerminalCard title="Survival Essentials">

The basics of staying alive in a world that wants you dead.

<details>
<summary>View Survival Guidelines</summary>

:::tip Core Guidelines
*   **Medical Prep**: Always carry bandages, painkillers, and stimpaks. Never leave safety without at least two of each.
*   **Sleep Management**: Exhaustion lethally lowers your carry weight and combat effectiveness. Identify safe houses early and plan your routes between them.
*   **Logistics**: Use the APC travel system and subway networks for safer, faster movement across the Commonwealth.
:::

</details>

</TerminalCard>

<TerminalCard title="Combat Tactics">

Fights in Anomaly are won before the first shot is fired.

<details>
<summary>View Tactical Rules</summary>

:::danger Tactical Rules
*   **Pre-Medication**: Use **TXA + Ibuprofen** before you enter a building or expect combat. This prevents critical bleeding and maintains your aim.
*   **Cover & Concealment**: NPCs will flank and use grenades. If you aren't behind a solid wall, you are a target.
*   **Ammunition Logic**: Only carry what you need. Ammo is heavy; bringing too much will leave you with no room for medical supplies.
:::

</details>

</TerminalCard>

<TerminalCard title="Resource & Gear Management">

Your equipment is your lifeline. Treat it with respect.

<details>
<summary>View Scavenging & Maintenance Tips</summary>

### Scavenging
*   **Junk is Life**: Every piece of scrap can be turned into essential materials for crafting and repairs.
*   **Water**: Fill empty bottles at pumps or clean water sources. Dehydration kills faster than hunger.

### Maintenance
*   **Armor Plates**: Regularly check your ballistic plates. A shattered plate offers zero protection against the next round.
*   **Weapon Wear**: Keep your firearms in top condition. Jamming during a firefight is usually fatal.

</details>

</TerminalCard>

<TerminalCard title="The Golden Rules of Radiation">

<details>
<summary>How to survive the invisible killer</summary>

1. **Avoid Hotspots**: If your Geiger counter starts clicking rapidly, back away. Do not "push through" without high-grade protection.
2. **Storm Protocol**: When a radiation storm hits, find shelter or equip a gas mask immediately.
3. **Meds First**: Treat radiation poisoning as soon as you are in a safe spot. Stacking debuffs will cripple your stats and eventually kill you.

</details>

</TerminalCard>

<TerminalCard title="Early Game Advice">

<details>
<summary>View New Survivor Advice</summary>

:::info For New Survivors
*   **Stay Local**: Don't rush into the city. Sanctuary and the surrounding forests provide enough resources to build a solid foundation.
*   **Build Safe Houses**: Turn available settlements into supply depots with clean water and a bed.
*   **Check the MCM**: Almost every system can be tuned to your personal preference via the Mod Configuration Menu.
:::

</details>

</TerminalCard>
