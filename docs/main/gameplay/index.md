# Gameplay guide

Fallout Anomaly transforms Fallout 4 into a hardcore survival experience with realistic combat, advanced needs systems, and deep customization.

## What's changed

:::info Core systems
- **Survival mechanics** — Advanced Needs 76 with hunger, thirst, hygiene, disease, and more
- **Medical system** — MAIM realistic injuries: bleeding, pain, limb damage, lethal headshots
- **Radiation** — Dangerous and deadly with stacking debuffs
- **Combat overhaul** — True Damage realistic weapon damage, smart enemy scaling
- **Skills & perks** — S7 Skills system for character progression
- **UI & controls** — Immersive HUD, extensive keybind customization
:::
