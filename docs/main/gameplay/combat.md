# Combat systems

Combat in Fallout Anomaly is tactical, lethal, and realistic. You cannot rely on high-HP pools or bullet-sponge mechanics. Survival depends on your equipment, your character's skills, and your tactical positioning.

---

<TerminalCard title="S7 Skills - Character Progression">

Character growth is managed via **[S7 Skills](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/62743)**. This system decouples perks from SPECIAL stats and links them directly to your active skill levels.

:::info Key Progression Features
*   **Tag Skills**: Choose starting specializations for significant early-game bonuses.
*   **Backgrounds**: Select your pre-Anomaly history to define your starting stats and gear.
*   **Skill Perks**: Powerful abilities unlock naturally as you level up your skills through use.
:::

</TerminalCard>

<TerminalCard title="True Damage - Realistic Lethality">

Anomaly utilizes **True Damage** to ensure that gunfights feel consistent and dangerous. Damage is determined by **caliber and projectile type**, not by magical weapon levels.

<details>
<summary>Explore Projectile & Damage Mechanics</summary>

### The Caliber System
In this system, all NPCs (including the player) have a baseline **90% damage reduction**. Weapon damage is then added back in based on the caliber's real-world lethality. 
*   **Consistency**: Every weapon using .38 rounds will deal similar, realistic damage.
*   **Caliber Matters**: A .50 Caliber round is devastating, regardless of which rifle fires it.

### Smart Tuning
*   **Caliber Scaling**: Handguns and rifles are balanced based on their barrel length and caliber.
*   **Sniper Buffs**: Dedicated sniper rifles receive significant boosts to range (+300%) and accuracy to reward long-range playstyles.
*   **Armor Plates**: High-end armor uses physical plates that can be shattered and replaced.

</details>

</TerminalCard>

<TerminalCard title="Munitions & SCOURGE">

To provide variety and unpredictability, we include expanded ammunition and randomized enemy scaling.

*   **[Munitions](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/66051)**: Adds dozens of new, lore-friendly ammo types like 9mm, .303, and specialized energy cells.
*   **[SCOURGE](https://www.nexusmods.com/fallout4/mods/60917)**: Randomizes enemy health and stats within logical ranges. You never know if that Deathclaw has 500 HP or if it's an apex predator with 900+ HP.

</TerminalCard>

<div style={{marginTop: '2rem'}}>

:::danger Combat Essentials
*   **Use Cover**: Trading fire in the open is a death sentence.
*   **Trigger Discipline**: Ammo is heavy and expensive. Make every shot count.
*   **Weak Point Targeting**: Headshots and unarmored limbs are the fastest way to end a fight.
*   **Tactical Retreat**: If you are bleeding and out of stamina, **leave**. Living to fight another day is the only victory that matters.
:::

</div>
