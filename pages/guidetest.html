What is Fallout Anomaly?

Fallout Anomaly is a true hardcore experience, bringing you as close to realism as possible with mods like Damn Apocalypse, MAIM, and more. Packed with new weapons and armor to face harsher challenges, Anomaly also features new enemy types, quests, settlement items, and a complete assortment of textures to elevate your game to modern standards. If you're searching for that gritty, harsh survival experience, Anomaly has you covered.

1. Survival Mechanics
🍽️ Survival Mode Features
Needs System: You must regularly eat, drink, and sleep to avoid penalties. Hunger, thirst, and fatigue will reduce your stats and eventually cause damage or even death
Fast Travel: Traditional fast travel is disabled. You can only fast travel by using the APC Travel System or navigating the expanded subway system found across the Commonwealth
Saving the Game: Manual saving and quicksaving are disabled. You can only save by sitting in a chair or using a Survival Pack item found in the Aid menu
Combat Difficulty: Both you and enemies deal more damage. Healing is slowed, and combat is more lethal across the board
Ammo Has Weight: Every bullet, shell, or fusion cell you carry adds weight to your inventory, forcing smarter inventory management
Illness & Injury: You can get sick, suffer broken limbs, or require items like antibiotics and splints for proper healing. Doctors may be needed to cure serious conditions
Carry Weight Reduced: Your base carry weight is significantly lower, making every item choice matter. Backpacks and perks help extend capacity
🎒 Backpacks

Backpacks can be found or crafted using perks from the Junk Jockey skill.

Note: The mods featured in the sections below also play a major role in shaping the survival experience. Be sure to explore them to get the full intended gameplay challenge.

Logical Ranges: Same-caliber weapons have logical damage ranges (e.g. .38 weapons do ~19–25 dmg)

5. Radiation System
⚛️ Radiation Mechanics
Dual-layered System: IRA (Ingested Radioactive Particles) from food/water/weather + RAD (Tissue Damage) from external exposure
Negative IRA Values: Deal internal damage over time and add to total IRA buildup
Acute Radiation Syndrome: High IRA levels cause periodic RAD damage and debilitating effects
Weather Hazards: Radiation storms require functional protective gear (gas masks, hazmat suits, power helmets)
Progressive Damage: Radiation left unchecked will eventually kill the player—even retreating won't stop progression unless treated
Breakable Gear: Protective gear is now breakable and must be maintained
Explosion Types: Differentiate between fusion and fission explosions—each behaves differently
Doctor Fees: Separate fees for small vs. large radiation treatments
RadBoy Tool: Optional tool that notifies players via text or voice when radiation or IRA levels rise dangerously
💊 Radiation Treatment Items
Ca-DTPA: Purges IRA. Removes 10 rads/sec for 60s (requires holstered weapon). Replaces RadAway and slowly removes IRA
Potassium Iodide: +200 RAD resistance and reduces RADs by 0.5/sec for 300s. Replaces Vanilla Rad-X
🏥 Disease Treatment Medications

Some conditions caused by radiation or exposure require specific medication:

Antibiotics: Cure infections
Sleep Aid: Treats insomnia (Warning: may be addictive)
Anodyne: Relieves fatigue
Anti-Parasitic: Removes parasites
Stimulants: Counters lethargy
Energy Pills: Mitigates weakness

Most of these medications can be purchased from doctors, or scavenged from raiders, gunners, and other NPCs. Be sure to stock up before venturing out.

⚠️ Survival Tips

The Wastes are unforgiving. Radiation is no longer a simple number—it's a slow, creeping death that can take hold in seconds or over days. Always carry enough protective gear and medication. Stock up on bandages, painkillers, Rad meds, and limb restoration tools. Being prepared isn't optional—it's survival.
